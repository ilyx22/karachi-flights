Before you book

  1. Flex the dates. Try Tue 9 → Tue 16 instead — Tuesdays often £30–50 cheaper.
  2. Middle East airspace. Qatar offers free date changes for tickets issued before 15 May 2026 with travel up to 15 Sep 2026.
  3. Baggage maths. Pegasus & Turkish basic don't include hold luggage. Etihad/Emirates include 30–35kg.
  4. Book now, not later. Sweet spot is ~27 days advance for this route.
  5. No direct flights. PIA only flies LHR to Islamabad and Lahore, not Karachi.
